Web Development

Full-stack developers do the work of both a back-end and front-end developer. These developers have the knowledge to build a complete website.A web developer’s job is to create websites. While their primary role is to ensure the website is visually appealing and easy to navigate, many web developers are also responsible for the website’s performance and capacity.

App Development

Mobile app developers are in charge of designing and developing mobile apps. Also the job role as a mobile app developer is to work with the client to create a video game, social networking app or any other type of software that will be used on a mobile phone or tablet.

UI/UX Design

UX designers create wireframes, and user flows, and design the overall look and feel of a product, while UX developers build the product, test it, and fix bugs. In short, UX designers design the look and feel of a product, and UX developers are responsible for the technical implementation.
